Project Value

Wine is one of the most fascinating thing in the world. When tasting great wines, we may often want to find similar ones. However, only a few of us finally put it into action – not everyone are well versed in wines. That’s our initial motivation to design such a webapp, to help people find the wine catered to their own taste, making them become a master of wine.

Data Preprocessing & Analysis

We downloaded the Wine Review data from Kaggle as our raw data, and used R language to clean up the data and find out the primary keys (used to uniquely match). Then we trained the Latent Dirichlet Allocation to generate topic models, thus finding out the topic groups behind 'description'. Besides, we visualized the cleaned data with Tableau, R and Python, and collected wine label images (to train & test our OCR model) from Internet with simple Python web crawler.
