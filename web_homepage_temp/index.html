Data Preprocessing

We use Wine Review data from Kaggle.
Our data cleansing steps include:
1. Remove countries with frequency less than 1000
2. Extract column year from column title
3. Remove duplicate rows and useless columns
4. Find out primary keys (used to uniquely match)
5. Merge rows group by "Title" and "Variety"
